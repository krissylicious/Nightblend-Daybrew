Our Story

The NightBlend & DayBrew is a partnership formed by Lois Cheney Brofar as the manager, Krislyn Mae Sinoy as production assistant, Trisha Pojas being the social media manager, Ma. Abegail and Kent Floyd Chuatico are the Baristas. While Robimel Chang is the assistant social media manager. The cornerstone of our partnership is fairness and equality. We firmly believe in ensuring an equitable distribution of shares and rights among our partners, fostering a sense of unity and shared ownership. This approach harmonizes with our belief that through partnership, we can cultivate a healthier work-life balance, creating a warm welcoming environment for both our customers and team members.
